**Project overview**

This project focuses on identifying the drivers of sales through rigorous data analysis. By integrating marketing data, the study evaluates factors such as price sensitivity, advertising efficiency. 

**Goals**

- Analyze the impact of marketing variables on sales revenue
- Develop a predictive model to forecast future sales revenue


**Tools**

  Data manipulation:
- Pandas
- Numpy

    Data visualization:
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
  
  predictive modeling
- SKlearn
